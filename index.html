Colorado

Denver

County of Denver

We flew in to Denver from Tucson, took the train from the airport to downtown and got out at 17th Street. It should have been easy to find our hotel in 14th Street. But 14th Street was the one street that didn't follow the grid... a dead end had us retracing our steps until we got help from some locals who were out for a night on the town.

A short evening stroll led us to a candy store but that was enough city for us so back to the hotel for a cuppa and early night, ready for a pre-dawn trip to the station to take the train to Granby.

California Zephyr from Denver to Granby

Granby

Grand County

The gorgeous little town of Granby in the Rocky Mountains where someone lucked out with their third pair of cowby boots, and where someone else thought they found US$100 on the side of the road. Staying at the WorldMark Granby Rocky Mountain Preserve, we spent a few days in tranquility, with crisp mornings followed by beautiful sunny days, and the occasional game of table tennis and billiards. There was a very convenient free bus, which we eventually worked out only went in one direction... hence we'd walk to town and catch the bus back so... lots of walking!
